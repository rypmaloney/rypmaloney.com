Hikenortheast

Hikenortheast.com is a demo hiking website. My goal was to create a platform where I could feature some of my favorite outdoor adventures, and provide tips and tricks for intermediate hiking near Boston. I wanted to practice the developent process from begininng to end, without the asstance of frameworks.

The site is written from scratch in HTML5 and CSS3. Functionality is provided with some basic Jquery. All photography, design, and copy are my own.

This project is a perpetual work in progress. I'm currently exploring tranisitioning it to Wordpress.

Slideshow

I put this slideshow together for the center director for a last-second meeting with a donor. I wrote the copy, composed the powerpoint and took the photos.

I think the most important guiding principle of a strong powerpoint presentation is a separation between the visual, and the spoken. No on enjoys watching the presenter read verbatim from the slides. Unfortunately, we didn’t have enough time to orchestrate an entire presentation, so this has more written content than I general aim for.
