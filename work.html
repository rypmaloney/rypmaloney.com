hikenortheast.com

Hikenortheast.com is a demo hiking website. My goal was to create a platform where I could feature some of my favorite outdoor adventures, and provide tips and tricks for intermediate hiking near Boston. I wanted to practice the developent process from begininng to end, without the asstance of frameworks.

The site is written from scratch in HTML5 and CSS3. Functionality is provided with some basic Jquery. All photography, design, and copy are my own.
