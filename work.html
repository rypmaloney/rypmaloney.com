Hikenortheast

Hikenortheast.com is a demo hiking blog. My goal was to create a platform where I could feature some of my favorite outdoor adventures, and provide tips and tricks for intermediate hiking near Boston. I wanted to practice the development process from beginning to end, without the assistance of frameworks.

The site is written from scratch in HTML5 and CSS3. Functionality is provided with some Jquery. All photography, design, and copy are my own.

This project is a perpetual work in progress. I'm currently exploring transitioning it to WordPress.

Slideshow

I put this slideshow together for the center director for a last-second meeting with a donor. I wrote the copy, composed the PowerPoint and took the photos.

I think the most important guiding principle of a strong PowerPoint presentation is a separation between the visual and the spoken. No one enjoys watching the presenter read verbatim from the slides. Unfortunately, we didn’t have enough time to orchestrate an entire presentation, so this has more written content than I generally would aim for.
